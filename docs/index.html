About The Project

Our team is currently working on the development of an innovative tool called Grizzly Paths, which aims to assist students at Georgia Gwinnett College in determining the most suitable classes to take in order to acquire the necessary skills for entry-level jobs in the field of Information Technology (IT). By providing students with a comprehensive overview of the job market and the skills required by employers, Grizzly Paths will enable them to make informed decisions when selecting their coursework. As we are still in the early stages of development, we are excited to showcase the first version of the website, along with the data we have collected, which demonstrates how we have correlated the jobs, skills, and courses. Through this endeavor, our goal is to empower Georgia Gwinnett College students with the knowledge and preparation they need to excel in their IT careers and secure meaningful employment opportunities.
